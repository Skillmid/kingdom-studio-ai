# ADR-0004: Adopt a Mobile-First Design Strategy

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-06-26

---

# Context

Kingdom Studio AI will serve creators worldwide.

Many creators, especially in emerging markets, begin their creative work on mobile devices before transitioning to desktop environments.

The platform should remain accessible regardless of device.

---

# Decision

Adopt a **mobile-first** design and development strategy.

Every feature will be designed for mobile before being expanded for tablet and desktop layouts.

---

# Why Mobile-First

Benefits include:

* Better accessibility
* Faster interface design
* Cleaner user experience
* Improved responsive layouts
* Broader audience reach

---

# Design Principles

Every screen should:

* Adapt gracefully to small screens.
* Preserve usability across devices.
* Avoid horizontal scrolling.
* Maintain readable typography.
* Keep touch interactions intuitive.

---

# Desktop Experience

Desktop layouts should enhance productivity by providing:

* Multi-panel workspaces
* Larger previews
* Rich editing interfaces
* Efficient navigation

The desktop experience builds upon the mobile foundation rather than replacing it.

---

# Consequences

Positive

* Consistent user experience
* Improved accessibility
* Better long-term maintainability

Trade-offs

* Additional planning required for complex desktop workflows
* Careful consideration needed for advanced editing features

---

# Future Review

Review this decision if:

* User research indicates different usage patterns.
* Desktop-specific workflows become significantly more important.
